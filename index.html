THE ENQUIRY DESK

HAVE QUESTIONS?

Who is eligible to apply for Season 1?

Lagos New Colossus is open to ambitious entrepreneurs between the ages of 25-40. Whether you have a revolutionary new idea or an existing business looking to scale, you are eligible to enter the crucible.

What is the total value of the Grand Prize?

The winner of Season 1 walks away with a war chest of N50,000,000 in cash and equity investment, plus a luxury suite in the Colossus ecosystem and three years of direct mentorship from our Titan partners.

Where do the regional auditions take place?

Auditions will be held physically in five key Lagos districts: Ikeja, Badagry, Ikorodu, Lagos Island, and Epe. Check the 'Audition Holds' section above for specific venue details once registration closes.

How does the selection process work?

Our Council of Lagos New Colossus evaluates applicants based on four pillars: Scalability, Execution, Audacity, and Impact. Candidates move from Auditions to an intensive Bootcamp before 12 finalists enter the House.

Do I need to be a Lagos resident to apply?

While the business must be viable within the Lagos ecosystem, we welcome the New Colossus from across all divisions of Lagos. However, finalists must be available to reside in the Colossus House for the duration of the 8-week production.
