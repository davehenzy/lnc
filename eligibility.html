THE CRITERIA
FOR LAGOS NEW COLOSSUS.

Before you apply, ensure you possess the steel required to compete at the highest level of industry.

AGE REQUIREMENT

Applicants must be between the ages of 18 and 40. This reflects our mission to empower the next generation of industry leaders who will sculpt Africa's economic destiny.

NIGERIAN IDENTITY

While the business must be viable within the Lagos ecosystem, we welcome entrepreneurs from across the state. You must hold valid identity documents for Nigerian citizenship.

BUSINESS FEASIBILITY

You must possess a revolutionary business idea or an existing startup ready to scale. Projects are evaluated on Scalability, Audacity, Execution, and Impact.

RESIDENCY

The 12 finalists will be required to reside in the Colossus House for the full 8-week production period. Absence from the crucible is grounds for immediate elimination.

ONLINE AUDITIONS

All candidates must submit a high-impact, 60-second video pitch. Your ability to communicate vision under pressure is the first test of your titanic potential.

CLEAN RECORD

Consistency is key. Applicants must have a record free from financial impropriety or ethical violations, as they represent the future pillars of the Lagos economy.
